Overview

Experion 151, Noida — The New Landmark of Luxury Living

Welcome to Experion 151, an ultra-luxury residential landmark spread across 5 acres of prime, 100% paid-up land directly allotted by the Noida Authority (E-Auction). This low-density development features three standalone towers rising S+35 to 38 floors, including one exclusive 4BHK tower, designed to offer privacy, open views, and architectural elegance. Every residence is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, showcasing grand entrance lobbies, fine finishes, and Grade A construction quality. The project also features a 50,000 sq. ft. grand clubhouse, offering curated lifestyle amenities, wellness zones, and leisure spaces that redefine luxury living. Experion 151 offers spacious 3BHK, 3BHK + Servant, and 4BHK residences ranging from 2100 sq. ft. to 3200 sq. ft., complemented by expansive balconies and panoramic skyline views. Priced attractively starting at ₹16,000 per sq ft* (+ other charges), it also comes with an attractive payment plan. From premium specifications to a serene environment, every element reflects sophistication and exclusivity. Limited inventory is now available with EOI priority access — making this the perfect opportunity to own a part of Noida’s most prestigious address, where every moment celebrates luxury and timeless living.
